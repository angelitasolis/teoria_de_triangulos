Types of Triangles

Equilateral Triangle

A triangle with all three sides of equal length. Angles are also equal, each measuring 60 degrees.

Learn More
Isosceles Triangle

A triangle with at least two sides of equal length. The angles opposite the equal sides are also equal.

Learn More
Scalene Triangle

A triangle with all sides of different lengths. No angles are equal.

Learn More
Right Triangle

A triangle with one 90-degree angle. The side opposite the right angle is called the hypotenuse.
